Selected Work

Product Design

MyHumana First Experience

MyHumana is a secure website that allows Humana members to access their claims, benefits, deductibles, and other personal plan and account information. For many members, MyHumana is the most common method of engagement with Humana.

Alexa: Experiments in Voice

Our team partnered with Humana’s innovation team on series of lean experiments to understand how voice technology might augment integrated care delivery and in-home care.

Personify: sharing research

Personify is an internal website for Humana associates to document and share user research. The site serves as a secure repository for researchers and stakeholders to have easy access to research data across products.

Creative Direction

Lennox Movember

Lennox ran a month-long social media campaign with the Movember Foundation to create awareness and support for men's health issues. This cause was a good fit not only because of the charitable giving, but also because the company’s iconic "mascot", Dave Lennox, has always featured a moustache.

The Louisville Paper

The Paper was a monthly magazine dedicated to celebrating all the good things happening in Louisville. The magazine had a strong focus on local arts, culture, and social activism.

Inside Actors: Humana Festival of New American Plays

A special interactive edition of the Inside Actors magazine for the 2011 Humana Festival of New American Plays.
